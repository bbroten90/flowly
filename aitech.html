The Problem with Traditional Verification

Invoice factoring has traditionally been a labor-intensive process, relying on manual review of documents and slow verification methods:

  • Days-long verification: Traditional factoring requires manual document review taking 2-5 business days.

  • Human error rate: Manual processes introduce inconsistencies and subjective judgment.

  • Limited fraud prevention: Traditional systems struggle to identify sophisticated fraud patterns.

  • Fixed pricing models: Risk assessment limitations force high, inflexible fees.

Technology Layers

Multi-Layered Verification System

Our proprietary AI verification technology uses four integrated layers

Document Analysis Layer

  • Computer vision OCR optimized for construction invoices
  • Entity recognition for business names, amounts, dates, services
  • Document classification (invoices, work orders, completion certificates)
  • Tampering detection to identify altered documents

Geo-Verification Layer

  • Location validation comparing work site coordinates with documentation
  • Timestamp verification ensuring photos were taken during expected timeframes
  • Device fingerprinting to validate consistent documentation sources
  • Weather data cross-referencing to validate outdoor work conditions

Cross-Document Intelligence

  • Semantic matching between work orders, invoices, and completion documents
  • Consistency verification across all submitted documentation
  • Historical pattern analysis comparing to previous submissions
  • Client signature validation with behavioral biometrics

Risk Assessment Engine

  • Dynamic scoring algorithm weighing multiple verification factors
  • Client history analysis incorporating payment records
  • Industry-specific risk models for different blue-collar sectors
  • Confidence scoring that directly influences factoring fees
