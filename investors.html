The Market Opportunity

Market Dynamics

The Payment Gap Problem

65% of blue-collar businesses report cash flow problems due to payment delays of 30-90 days, affecting their ability to grow and operate efficiently.

Growing Sector

The construction, trades, and field service sectors are growing at 8% annually, creating increasing demand for cash flow solutions.

Underbanked Segment

Traditional financial institutions struggle to serve blue-collar businesses effectively, creating an opportunity for specialized fintech solutions.
